Banana Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana

  • 250ml milk

  • 3 ice cubes

    • Method:

  • 1. Peel the banana and add to a blender

  • 2. Add the milk and ice cubes to the blender

  • 3. Turn on the blender for 30 seconds

  • 4. Drink

    • Spaghetti Bolognese


    Ingredients:


  • 1 onion

  • 1 clove of garlic

  • 1lb of mince

  • 2 tins of tomatoes

  • Black pepper and salt

  • herbs

  • A splash of red wine

  • Tomato puree

    • Method:

  • 1. Brown the mince in a large pan

  • 2. Fry the chopped onions

  • 3. Add the tinned tomatoes then add the tomato puree

  • 4. Season to taste

  • 5. Add a glug of red wine and herbs

  • 6. In a separate pan cook the spaghetti for 10 mins in boiling water

  • 7. Serve, eat and enjoy with grated cheeese

    • Tomato and Lentil Soup


    Ingredients:


  • 400g of lentils

  • 1 tin of tomatoes

  • 1 onion and a clove of garlic

  • 1 carrot

  • 1 L veg stock

  • 1 tsp of cumin

    • Method:

  • 1. Fry the onion and garlic

  • 2. Add all the other ingredients

  • 3. Simmer for 15-20 mins

  • 4. Season to taste and whizz till smooth

  • 5. Eat and enjoy
