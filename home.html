    Banana Milkshake

    Ingredients:

  • 1 banana

  • 250ml milk

  • 3 ice cubes

    • Method:

  • 1. Peel the banana and add to a blender

  • 2. Add the milk and ice cubes to the blender

  • 3. Turn on blender for 30 seconds

  • 4. Drink

    • Chocolate milkshake

    This scrummy high-cal milk shake can start your day with a SMACK

    Ingredients and needed resources:


  • A glass(a big one is better)

  • Semi-skimmed milk

  • UHT whipped cream to taste

  • 3 Heaped Teaspoons of drinking chocolate

    • Method:

  • 1. Fill your glass 2/3 full with cold milk

  • 2. Put the teaspoons of chocolate powder on top

  • 3. Then put the UHT cream(scream as you do it) and fill the glass to the top

  • 4. stir for 30 seconds

  • 5. leave to stand for 2 mins

  • 6. stir again (THE MORE YOU STIR THE MORE FROTHY IT GETS!)

  • 7. Drink and enjoy 😀🥛🧁🥤🥤🥤🥤🥤🍺🍻

    • AjÍ (A tomato salsa with chilli)

    Ingredients:

  • Some juice from one lime
