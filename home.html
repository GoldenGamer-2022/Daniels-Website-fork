    Banana Milkshake

    Ingredients:

  • 1 banana

  • 250ml milk

  • 3 ice cubes

    • Method:

  • 1. peel the banana and add to a blender

  • 2. Add the milk and icecubes to the blender

  • 3. turn on blender for 30 seconds

  • 4. Drink
